THE LEGACY

REDEFINING AUTOMOTIVE EXCELLENCE

Established in 2026 in Ahmedabad, Edelhaus Automotive has quickly become the pinnacle of luxury car showrooms in India. We don't just sell cars — we curate the world's most exclusive automotive masterpieces.

Every vehicle in our collection is hand-selected, inspected against our rigorous 150-point Edelhaus Certification, and presented in a showroom designed to match the grandeur of the automobiles inside.

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Global Sourcing

We utilize a vast global network to source rare hypercars and limited-edition models directly from Japan, the UK, and Dubai — often before they reach the open market.

Edelhaus Certified

Every vehicle undergoes a rigorous 150-point Edelhaus Certification, ensuring that performance, luxury and authenticity are never compromised — ever.

White Glove Experience

Our dedicated consultants provide a fully personalized journey — from private showroom viewings to bespoke door-step delivery across Gujarat and beyond.
